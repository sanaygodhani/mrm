Welcome

Simple, dependable sourcing

At Mishal Reborn Metals we make sourcing metal scrap simple and dependable. Based in Surat, Gujarat, we bring carefully selected material from trusted European yards to local manufacturers and recyclers. Our tone is friendly, our process is transparent, and our goal is to make every customer feel confident from first contact to delivery.


About Us

Company with seasoned roots

Mishal Reborn Metals is a company with roots in multiple years of hands-on market work. We started by learning the trade, building relationships with scrap yards and buyers, and refining how to sort and grade material so every lot we move meets real needs. Now we bring that experience to customers who want a partner, not just a supplier.

Our Approach

We treat every shipment as a promise. That means careful sourcing, honest grading, and clear paperwork. We value long-term partnerships and aim to make every transaction easy, predictable, and fair.

Where We Operate

Our sorting and inspection facility is in Surat, Gujarat, positioned to serve India's manufacturing hubs. We import primarily from Belgium and the Netherlands, with occasional purchases from Germany.

Our Products

Five metals, one reliable source

Al
Aluminium Scrap
Lightweight and highly recyclable, our aluminium is cleaned and sorted for remelting and extrusion. We focus on low contamination and consistent grades so foundries and extrusion mills can rely on predictable alloy behavior.
Br
Brass Scrap
Brass lots are separated by type and cleanliness to maximize recovery value. We supply turnings, sheet, and mixed brass suitable for refineries and component manufacturers who need steady, well-documented feedstock.
Cu
Copper Scrap
We provide bare bright, insulated, and mixed copper grades. Each lot is prepared to reduce impurities and improve furnace efficiency, helping electrical and cable manufacturers get the most value from recycled copper.
Fe
Iron Scrap
Cast and wrought iron are sorted by size and composition. Our iron scrap is prepared for foundries and steelmakers who need consistent, contaminant-free feedstock.
St
Steel Scrap
From structural pieces to sheet and turnings, our steel scrap is graded by form and carbon content. We offer bundles and loose loads to match mill requirements and help optimize melt cycles.

Quality First

Every lot is visually inspected and sampled at our Surat facility. We provide clear labeling and basic composition checks so buyers know exactly what they're receiving — before the shipment ever leaves our floor.

Sustainable Practice

We believe in the circular economy. By responsibly sourcing and moving scrap back into manufacturing, we reduce waste and support sustainable production for India's growing industrial sector.
